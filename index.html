What Is MetroYatra?

MetroYatra is a fast, accurate, and easy-to-use metro route planning service designed for India’s expanding metro networks. It helps commuters explore the best routes, platform information, gate details, and travel time estimates with ease.

Key Features

Smart Route Finding

Find the fastest, shortest, and most efficient metro routes between stations.

Platform Information

Know boarding platforms, interchange levels, and transfer paths instantly.

Gate Details

Exit gate numbers with nearby landmarks for smoother station navigation.

Travel Time Estimates

Route duration including interchanges and walking time.

Multiple Route Options

Choose options with fewer interchanges or shortest walking distance.

SEO-Friendly Route URLs

Clean, shareable link formats for each metro route.
